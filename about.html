Niamh Crisp O'Brien | About
Youth Voice
Niamh has been a member of Surrey Youth Cabinet for several years now. She has worked on a climate conference, eco-travel and green eating. In 2022, she got elected as MYP, working towards a better climate education in Surrey.
Climate Activism
Niamh started her climate activism when she was 11, inspired by Greta Thurnberg. She had since become a member of Teach The Future, an organisation who have sucessfully campaigned for better climate education in schools. She is also a member of Teach the Teacher, an organisation set up to help students teach their teachers about the climate crisis!
Real Niamh
Outside of her outstanding youth voice work, Niamh also has a very active life. She enjoys going to concerts, listening to music, cycling and walking around her local area and meeting up with friends. She loves Percy Pigs, and is also gluten free.
