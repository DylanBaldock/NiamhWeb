Niamh Crisp O'Brien

Niamh is an award-winning youth activist based in the heart of Surrey. She spends her time talking to MPs, writing draft policy and meeting her constituents.


Outside of her youth work, she enjoys getting out in the Surrey Hills, going to concerts and being gluten-free. Niamh is currently in her first year at Godalming College, studying A-Levels in something boring, something fun, and 2 langauges if I can remember correctly.
